Fluffy French Toast

Description

This French toast recipe is different because it uses flour. I have given it to some friends and they've all liked it better than the French toast they usually make!

123 calories; protein 4.8g; carbohydrates 19.4g; fat 2.7g; cholesterol 48.1mg; sodium 230.2mg.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoons white sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 12 slices thick bread
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. IMeasure flour into a large mixing bowl. Slowly whisk in the milk. Whisk in the salt, eggs, cinnamon, vanilla extract and sugar until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium heat.
  3. Soak bread slices in mixture until saturated. Cook bread on each side until golden brown. Serve hot.
